About Us

Who We Are

Study Sync is a collaborative learning platform built for students who want to study smarter—not harder. We bring together modern tools, real collaboration, and personalized guidance to make learning more effective and enjoyable.

Our Mission

We aim to empower students with a platform that blends study planning, group collaboration, doubt solving, and motivation—so that no student ever feels alone in their learning journey.
